Rich Dad Poor Dad

by Robert T. Kiyosaki

Genre: Personal Finance

Publisher: Warner Books

Published Date: April 1, 2000

Page Count: 207 pages

About the Book

"Rich Dad Poor Dad" is a groundbreaking book that challenges conventional wisdom about money and investing. Robert Kiyosaki shares his personal experiences from two fathers—his biological father (Poor Dad) and his best friend's father (Rich Dad)—to illustrate the differences in mindset and financial strategies. The book emphasizes the importance of financial education, investing in assets, and understanding how money works to achieve financial independence and success.
